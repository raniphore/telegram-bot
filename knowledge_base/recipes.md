# Quick Recipes

## Classic Pasta Carbonara
**Serves:** 2 | **Time:** 20 minutes

**Ingredients:**
- 200g spaghetti
- 100g pancetta or bacon
- 2 large eggs
- 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
- 2 cloves garlic
- Salt and black pepper

**Instructions:**
1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
2. Fry pancetta with garlic until crispy.
3. Whisk eggs with Parmesan and black pepper.
4. Drain pasta, reserving 1/2 cup pasta water.
5. Off heat, mix pasta with pancetta, then egg mixture. Add pasta water to loosen.
6. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan.

## Avocado Toast
**Serves:** 1 | **Time:** 5 minutes

**Ingredients:**
- 2 slices sourdough bread
- 1 ripe avocado
- Lemon juice, salt, chili flakes

**Instructions:**
1. Toast the bread.
2. Mash avocado with lemon juice and salt.
3. Spread on toast, top with chili flakes.

## Overnight Oats
**Serves:** 1 | **Time:** 5 minutes prep + overnight

**Ingredients:**
- 80g rolled oats
- 200ml milk or plant-based milk
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 1 tbsp honey
- Toppings: berries, banana, nuts

**Instructions:**
1. Mix oats, milk, chia seeds, and honey in a jar.
2. Refrigerate overnight.
3. Top with fresh fruit and nuts before serving.
