# Company Policy

## Working Hours
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Flexible working arrangements are available with manager approval.

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Remote work requires a stable internet connection and availability during core hours (10 AM – 3 PM).

## Leave Policy
- Annual leave: 20 days per year
- Sick leave: 10 days per year (medical certificate required after 3 consecutive days)
- Public holidays: As per local government calendar

## Code of Conduct
All employees must treat colleagues with respect. Harassment or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated and may result in disciplinary action.

## Data Privacy
Employees must not share confidential company or customer data with unauthorized parties. Violations may result in termination and legal action.

## Equipment Policy
Company-issued equipment is for work purposes only. Report lost or stolen devices immediately to IT support.

## Expense Reimbursement
Submit expense claims within 30 days of purchase with receipts. Pre-approval required for expenses over $100.
