# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is this bot?
This is a hybrid AI bot that can answer questions from a knowledge base and describe images.

## How do I ask a question?
Use the /ask command followed by your question. Example: `/ask What are the working hours?`

## How do I get an image described?
Simply send a photo to the bot and it will automatically generate a caption and keyword tags.

## What topics does the knowledge base cover?
The knowledge base contains information about company policies, tech FAQs, and general recipes.

## Is my data stored?
No user data or images are stored. Queries are processed in real-time.

## What AI model powers this bot?
The bot uses OpenAI GPT-4o-mini for both text generation and image analysis. Embeddings are generated locally using all-MiniLM-L6-v2.
